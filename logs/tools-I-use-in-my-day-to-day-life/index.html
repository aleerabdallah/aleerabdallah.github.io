Tools I Use in My Day-to-Day Life

Abdallah | 2025-08-25

As a developer and content creator, the tools I use every day shape how I work. Here's a curated list of what I rely on regularly — from productivity apps to development environments.

🧠 Productivity

  • Notion – My go-to for notes, task management, and idea organization.
  • Obsidian – For daily journaling and connected thought writing (Zettelkasten-style).
  • Raycast – Lightning-fast launcher and command center for macOS.
  • Google Calendar – Simple but effective for scheduling.

💻 Development

  • VS Code – My primary code editor with a few essential extensions:
  • Prettier
  • ESLint
  • GitLens
  • iTerm2 – Terminal of choice with custom themes and Zsh.
  • Docker – For containerizing projects and running services locally.
  • GitHub Copilot – For quick code suggestions and prototyping.

🌐 Browsing & Extensions

  • Brave Browser – Fast, secure, and privacy-conscious.
  • Pocket – Save articles to read later.
  • Tab Wrangler – Helps keep open tabs under control.

🎨 Design & Visuals

  • Figma – Collaborative design for UI/UX mockups.
  • Pixelmator Pro – Lightweight image editing on macOS.
  • LottieFiles – Quick animations for websites and apps.

☁️ Cloud & Backup

  • Dropbox – Simple cloud storage and syncing.
  • Backblaze – Automated, affordable cloud backup.
  • 1Password – Password manager I fully trust.

🎵 Focus & Music

  • Spotify – With tons of playlists for coding, writing, and deep work.
  • Noisli – Ambient sound generator for focus.

🔚 Final Thoughts

The specific tools might change over time, but the principles remain the same: simplicity, speed, and reliability.

Have a favorite tool I didn’t mention? Let me know!
